This cheat sheet is for a single Standard LION build (NFS).
Standard LION builds contain 3 volumes: s0xorax_snap, s0xoraadmx_snap, and n0xorax_nosnap
If volumes use new standard naming, check the qtrees to identify the volumes.
s0xorax volume hosts s0xoradatax qtree. s0xoraadm hosts s0xoraadminx qtree. n0xora will have n0xora qtrees
Do not use this template if your migration does not follow this standard.
